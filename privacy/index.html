Privacy Policy

Last updated: July 7, 2026

This Privacy Policy explains how Tent Makers Constructions and Developments, doing business as Tent Makers Group ("Tent Makers Group," "we," "us," or "our"), collects, uses, and protects your information when you visit our website, contact us, or use our services.

Information We Collect

  • Contact information you provide — name, email address, phone number, and mailing address.
  • Project details and any message content you send us through our forms, chat, phone, or email.
  • Usage data such as pages visited, collected automatically to improve our website.

How We Use Your Information

  • To respond to your inquiries and provide quotes and services.
  • To schedule and manage appointments and projects.
  • To send you service updates, appointment reminders, and — where you have opted in — occasional offers.
  • To improve our website, services, and customer support.

SMS / Text Messaging

By providing your mobile phone number and opting in (for example, by checking the SMS consent box on our contact form or by texting us), you consent to receive text messages from Tent Makers Group related to your inquiry, appointments, service and project updates, customer care, and occasional offers.

Message frequency varies. Message and data rates may apply. Reply STOP to unsubscribe at any time. Reply HELP for help, or contact us at (689) 308-7375. Consent to receive text messages is not a condition of any purchase.

No mobile information will be shared with third parties or affiliates for marketing or promotional purposes. Information sharing with subcontractors in support services, such as customer service, is permitted. All other categories exclude text messaging originator opt-in data and consent; this information will not be shared with any third parties.

How We Share Information

We do not sell your personal information. We may share information with trusted service providers who help us operate our business (such as scheduling, messaging, and customer-service tools), only as needed to provide our services and subject to confidentiality obligations. We may disclose information where required by law.

Data Retention & Security

We retain personal information only as long as necessary for the purposes described above or as required by law, and we use reasonable safeguards to protect it.

Your Choices

You may opt out of text messages by replying STOP, unsubscribe from emails using the link provided, or contact us to request access to or deletion of your information.
