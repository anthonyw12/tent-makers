About Tent Makers Group

Tent Makers Group is the trade name of Tent Makers Constructions and Developments, a Florida corporation headquartered in Winter Garden, Florida. We serve business and residential clients throughout the greater Orlando area.

Our team brings construction, development and business-growth services under one roof. Whether you need a space built or a business built up, we manage the project end to end and keep you informed every step of the way.
